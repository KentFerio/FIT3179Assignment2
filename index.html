Australia’s Life Expectancy in a Global Context

This dashboard explores Australia’s life expectancy and health outcomes in comparison with global patterns. It begins with a worldwide overview before zooming in to examine differences across Australian states and territories.

Why Compare Australia with the World?

Australia consistently ranks among the countries with the highest life expectancy. Understanding how it compares to global averages helps highlight the factors that contribute to this strong performance — from economic growth to health system stability.

Key Global Insights

  • Positive Relationship: Countries with higher GDP per capita tend to have longer life expectancy.
  • Australia’s Position: Australia sits high on both scales, reflecting strong healthcare and living standards.
  • Regional Gaps: Developing regions in Africa and Asia still lag behind in health outcomes despite gradual improvements.
